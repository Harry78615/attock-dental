Dr. Mahwash Khizran is a fully qualified Dental Surgeon with advanced specialist training in orthodontics (D-Ortho). She leads Attock Dental Clinic with a hands-on, personalised approach — ensuring every patient receives expert care tailored to their specific needs.

She offers general dentistry, scaling and polishing, teeth whitening, orthodontic treatments (braces and aligners), simple tooth extractions, restorative care, and paediatric dentistry.
